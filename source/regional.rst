AMP Regional Platform
========================

**Site Management**

1. Click "Sites" to open the sites page

.. image:: _static/media/image6.png
   :width: 6.5in
   :height: 3.37222in


2. On the “Sites” page, you can

-  View the sites in map view by clicking the **Map View** button

-  Filter the sites by status by clicking the filter buttons

-  Click on a site name to see more details

.. image:: _static/media/image3.png
   :width: 6.5in
   :height: 3.29722in


.. image:: _static/media/image7.png
   :width: 6.5in
   :height: 3.37569in


.. image:: _static/media/image5.png
   :width: 6.5in
   :height: 3.37222in


3. On the single-site page, you can:

-  Print the site report by clicking the **Print/PDF** button

-  Click the toggle buttons to see more information

**Document Management**

1. Click "Documents".

.. image:: _static/media/image8.png
   :width: 6.5in
   :height: 3.37222in

2. Click:

-  **New upload:** to upload a new document

-  **New Category:** to create a new category

.. image:: _static/media/image1.png
   :width: 6.5in
   :height: 3.29722in

3. Fill in the document form, then click **Upload Document** to submit
      the document and its details.

..

   .. image:: _static/media/image4.png
      :width: 6.5in
      :height: 3.37222in

4. If the upload is successful, you will be redirected to the documents
      page, where your document will appear among the documents on the
      page.

..

   .. image:: _static/media/image2.png
      :width: 6.5in
      :height: 3.37187in

5. On the documents page, you can hover over a single document and
      click:

-  The **Download icon** to download a document

-  The **Delete icon** to delete a document

**Indicators**

1. Click “Indicators” to open the QAMF Dashboard of Indicators.

..

   .. image:: _static/media/image10.png
      :width: 6.5in
      :height: 3.20833in

2. On the Dashboard of Indicators, you can:

-  Toggle categories by changing the QAMF categories

-  Compare countries by selecting the countries to compare

-  View historical evolution by clicking the “Evolution” button

..

   .. image:: _static/media/image9.png
      :width: 6.5in
      :height: 3.20833in

3. On clicking the “Evolution” button, you will be redirected to the
      QAMF trends page, where you can:

-  View monthly trends by clicking the “Energy (12 months)" button.

-  Select a specific country to see country-specific information

-  Go back to the “Dashboard of Indicators” page by clicking "Current
      Indicators".

..

   .. image:: _static/media/image11.png
      :width: 6.5in
      :height: 3.20833in
