Manage users
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Invite user
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
1. On the homepage click the dropdown button with your organization name.

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/organization_management_home.png

2. Click ``MANAGE`` and select ``Invite User``

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/organization_with_manage_clicked.png

3. Fill in the form provided and click ``INVITE``.

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/invite_user.jpg

Update user privileges
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^
1. On the homepage click the dropdown button with your organization name

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/organization_management_home.png

2. Click |key| to open the privileges form

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/click_privilege_key.png

3. Edit the form provided to change the user's privileges

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/privileges_form.png

4. Click ``UPDATE PRIVILEGES`` at the bottom of the form to save

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/update_priviledges.png

Remove user
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

1. On the homepage click the dropdown button with your organization name

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/organization_management_home.png

2. Click |user-minus| to remove a user

.. image:: ../_static/regional/organizations/click_remove_user.png
